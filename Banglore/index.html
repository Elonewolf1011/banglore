About Bangalore

Bangalore, officially known as Bengaluru, is the capital city of the Indian state of Karnataka. It is located in the southern part of India, on the Deccan Plateau. Bangalore is often referred to as the "Silicon Valley of India" due to its thriving information technology industry.

History

Bangalore has a rich and diverse history that dates back over a thousand years. It was founded by Kempe Gowda I in the 16th century and has since witnessed the rule of various dynasties including the Western Gangas, Cholas, Hoysalas, and the Vijayanagara Empire. The city has evolved into a major cultural, economic, and technological hub.

Attractions

Bangalore offers a wide range of attractions for visitors, including:

  • Lalbagh Botanical Garden
  • Cubbon Park
  • Bangalore Palace
  • Vidhana Soudha
  • ISKCON Temple
  • Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace
